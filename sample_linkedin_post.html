Dr. Anik Nandi's scholarly work, spanning nearly two decades, on family and societal multilingualism has been featured in the 3rd HaBilNet Colloquium's website. He spoke about how language practices within Indian transnational families can foster well-being, self-esteem, and cultural pride—especially when multilingualism is embraced as a resource rather than a challenge.
